Book Information

FBI agent Leo Veyne is drawn into a case that feels too personal — a string of precise, brutal crimes tied to a silent manipulator named Silas.

As the investigation deepens, it begins to unravel Leo himself. The past is circling back, piece by piece. And in a world shaped by betrayal, the truth isn't just dangerous — it's personal.

NEMESIS is a psychological thriller about identity, betrayal, and the quiet war between who we are and who we used to be.
